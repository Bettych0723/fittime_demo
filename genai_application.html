GenAI application

Pilot generative AI applications in a controlled, practical way.

What we do

We help identify practical GenAI use cases (customer Q&A bots, document summarization, report drafting, marketing copy seeds) and create low-risk pilot environments: prompt strategy, model integration (API, fine-tuning, or RAG), and a minimal viable prototype, alongside privacy/compliance checks and output review workflows. The emphasis is on evaluating benefits and risks in a controlled manner and documenting reproducible experiment steps.

Prompt design and governance

Precise prompt engineering with controlled output

Rapid prototyping

Fast prototyping for chatbots, summarizers, or similar use cases

Privacy and compliance

Robust data privacy safeguards and full regulatory compliance

Use-case mapping and prompt templates

Map business use cases and provide tailored prompt templates to accelerate adoption.

Minimal working prototype (bot or summarizer)

Deliver a functional prototype (e.g., chatbot or summarizer) to quickly validate value.

RAG pipeline design or recommendation

Design or recommend a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline to optimize knowledge retrieval and generation.

API / LLM APIs

Integrate OpenAI, Anthropic, and Hugging Face with flexible switching for high-quality generative output.

Orchestration framework

Google Android AR platform with real-time object and spatial tracking.

Vector databases

Leverage Pinecone or SQLite+FAISS for semantic search, long-term memory, and document retrieval.

Application and deployment layer

Build APIs with FastAPI or Flask and deploy via Docker for rapid, scalable rollout.

Who it’s for

Teams in customer service, content ops, marketing, or legal that handle high text volumes and seek efficiency gains.
